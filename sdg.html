Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

SDG Rules for Water Management in Jaipur

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) play a vital role in ensuring the long-term sustainability of water management practices in Jaipur. The following SDGs are particularly relevant to this project:

  • SDG 6 - Clean Water and Sanitation: Ensuring access to safe water for all and managing water resources sustainably.
  • SDG 13 - Climate Action: Addressing the challenges posed by climate change through improved water conservation measures.
  • SDG 11 - Sustainable Cities and Communities: Developing cities that are resilient to climate impacts and use resources efficiently.
  • SDG 3 - Good Health and Well-being: Ensuring that clean water and sanitation are available to improve public health outcomes.

These SDGs guide the city's water management strategies, ensuring that efforts to address water scarcity are aligned with global sustainability objectives. The implementation of these rules also facilitates the creation of a resilient water system that can withstand future challenges.
